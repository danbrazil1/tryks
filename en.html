Privacy Policy — Tryks

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Summary: Tryks is a local-first app. All of your data is stored exclusively on your device. We have no server, we do not require a login, and Tryks does not collect, transmit, or sell any of your personal data.

1. Who we are

Tryks is an app developed by Daniel Cavalcante to help you organize and learn kitesurfing tricks and tricks from other board sports, using public videos from Instagram and YouTube.

For any questions about this policy, contact us by e-mail: tryks@cavalcante.info.

2. What data the app collects

None. Tryks does not collect, transmit, or store off your device any personal information, identifiers, location, contacts, telemetry, usage data, or logs. The app also shows no ads and includes no advertising or analytics SDKs.

3. What data stays on your device

The app stores the following locally, in an SQLite database within the app's own private storage:

  • The links (URLs) of the videos you add;
  • Descriptions, notes, tags, and sport categories you create;
  • Your learning progress (status, favorites, difficulty);
  • Public information about the videos — title, description, and the author's name/profile — as displayed by the platforms themselves, used to credit the creators;
  • Cached video thumbnails (so they don't have to be loaded again).

This data never leaves your device, except when you voluntarily export it through the in-app backup feature (Configurações → Bases). In that case, the generated file is under your control: you decide whether to share it, save it to your personal cloud, send it to a friend, and so on.

4. Permissions requested

  • Internet: to load the Instagram and YouTube videos inside the app — the same public content you would see in a browser.
  • Receive a shared link: so you can use "Share → Tryks" from other apps.
  • Save and open files: only to export and import your backup, to the file you choose, when you request it.

5. Third-party content

Videos are shown through the official Instagram and YouTube players (via WebView). When you watch a video inside Tryks, those services may collect their own data, in accordance with their respective privacy policies:

  • Google / YouTube Privacy Policy
  • Instagram (Meta) Privacy Policy

Tryks has no access to, control over, or visibility into the data that Instagram and YouTube may collect while displaying their own content.

6. Children

Tryks is not directed at children. Because the app does not collect data from any user, there is no collection or processing of data from children or adolescents.

7. Security

Since no data is transmitted to or stored on our servers, there is no risk of a leak caused by a failure on our side. The security of the data that stays on your device depends on Android's own protections (PIN, biometrics, device encryption).

8. Your rights

Since we do not hold your data, there is no access, correction, or deletion request to make to us. To erase the app's local data, simply:

  • Use the "Apagar tudo" (Delete all) option in the app, under Configurações → Bases; or
  • Uninstall the app, which removes the local database along with it.

9. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in the future, the new version will be published at this same URL, with the revised update date shown at the top.

10. Contact

Questions, suggestions, or privacy concerns: tryks@cavalcante.info.
