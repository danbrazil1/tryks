Privacy Policy — Kitetricks

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Summary: Kitetricks is a local-first application. All your data is stored exclusively on your device. We have no server, no login, and we do not collect, transmit or sell any of your personal data.

1. Who we are

Kitetricks is a personal application developed by Daniel Cavalcante to organize and learn kitesurfing tricks from public videos on Instagram and YouTube.

For any questions about this policy, please contact: daniel@cavalcante.info.

2. What data the app collects

None. The application does not collect, transmit or store outside your device any personal information, identifiers, location, contacts, telemetry, usage data or logs.

3. What data stays on your device

The app stores locally, in a SQLite database within the app's private storage:

  • The links (URLs) of the videos you add;
  • Nicknames, notes, tags and disciplines you create;
  • Your learning progress (status, favorites, difficulty);
  • Cached video thumbnails (to avoid re-downloading).

This data never leaves your device, except when you voluntarily export it through the app's "Backup" feature — in that case, the generated JSON file is under your control (share, save to personal cloud, send to a friend, etc.).

4. Permissions requested

  • Internet: to load Instagram and YouTube videos inside the app (the same public content you would see in a browser).
  • Receive shared link: so you can use "Share → Kitetricks" from other apps.
  • Storage (write/read file): only to export and import your backup when you request it.

5. Third-party content

Videos are displayed through the official Instagram and YouTube players (via WebView). When you watch a video inside Kitetricks, those services may collect their own data according to their respective privacy policies:

  • Google / YouTube Privacy Policy
  • Instagram (Meta) Privacy Policy

Kitetricks has no access, control or visibility over the data that Instagram and YouTube may collect while displaying their own content.

6. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not intentionally collect data from children. Since nothing is collected from anyone, there is no specific risk in this regard.

7. Security

Since no data is transmitted or stored on servers, there is no risk of leakage caused by us. The security of the data on your device depends on Android's own protections (PIN, biometrics, device encryption).

8. Your rights

Since we do not have your data, there is no access, correction or deletion request to be made to us. To delete the app's local data:

  • Use the "Apagar tudo" / "Delete everything" option in Settings → Danger zone; or
  • Uninstall the application, which removes the local database as well.

9. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in the future, the new version will be published at this same URL with the revised update date at the top.

10. Contact

Questions, suggestions or concerns about privacy: kitetricks@cavalcante.info.

Kitetricks · Developed by Daniel Cavalcante · Local-first, no data collection.
